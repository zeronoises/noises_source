&nbsp;

### I aim to play:

&nbsp;

### at least one tune that you've always loved,  
### at least one tune that you'd forgotten you love,  
### at least one tune you've never heard before but now love,  
### & at least one tune that you hate...

&nbsp;

### ...but always with love.
